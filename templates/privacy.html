We are committed to ensure that the website of IIT-Bombay, Aakash Team is accessible to all users irrespective of device in use, technology or ability. As a result, this website can be viewed from a variety of devices, such as web-enabled mobile devices, wap phones, PDAs, and so on. We have put in our best efforts to ensure that all information on this website is accessible to people with disabilities.
